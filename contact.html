FAQ


How can I be sure the user is who they say they are?

We ask all our users when they first sign up to email us a piece of government issued ID (with personal details hidden if they wish). These users will receive a verified tag. If you suspect that other users may not be who they say they are then feel free to only deal with verified uers.


How does here2share ensure my safety when meeting other users for an exchange?

Since we have no control over what our users do outside our website, we encourage our users who are planning to meet to carry out an exchange to take all sensible safety precautions just as they would in using dating apps. Meet only in public places, and bring along a friend and of course let friends/family know where you will be.
